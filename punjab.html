Famous Places in Punjab

Golden Temple

A spiritual and architectural marvel in Amritsar, also known as Harmandir Sahib, a holy place for Sikhs worldwide.

Jallianwala Bagh

A historic garden in Amritsar, commemorating the tragic massacre of 1919 with a memorial and museum.

Wagah Border

Experience the patriotic retreat ceremony at the India-Pakistan border near Amritsar.

Qila Mubarak

A stunning fort complex in Patiala showcasing traditional Sikh architecture and rich history.

Anandpur Sahib

A sacred Sikh pilgrimage site, known for its serene Gurudwara and historical significance.

Rural Museum

Located at Punjab Agricultural University, this museum showcases rural life and culture in Punjab.
