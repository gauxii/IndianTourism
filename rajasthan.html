Famous Places in Rajasthan

Hawa Mahal

An architectural marvel in Jaipur, also known as the Palace of Winds, with its intricate latticework facade.

Jaisalmer Fort

Known as the Golden Fort, this living fort in Jaisalmer is a UNESCO World Heritage site and a historic gem.

Lake Palace

A beautiful palace located in the middle of Lake Pichola in Udaipur, now a luxury hotel.

Mehrangarh Fort

A massive fort in Jodhpur, known for its impressive architecture and stunning views of the Blue City.

Brahma Temple

A rare temple dedicated to Lord Brahma, located near the sacred Pushkar Lake.

Ranthambore National Park

A famous wildlife sanctuary, home to the majestic Bengal tiger and other exotic species.
