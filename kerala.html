Famous Places in Kerala

Munnar

A hill station known for its tea gardens, rolling hills, and breathtaking views of the Western Ghats.

Alleppey (Alappuzha)

Known as the "Venice of the East," it offers serene backwaters and houseboat cruises.

Wayanad

A district filled with lush greenery, waterfalls, wildlife sanctuaries, and trekking trails.

Thekkady

Famous for the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, spice plantations, and serene lakes.

Kochi (Cochin)

A historic port city known for its Chinese fishing nets, Fort Kochi, and colonial architecture.

Kovalam

A stunning beach destination offering crescent-shaped beaches and a vibrant nightlife.
