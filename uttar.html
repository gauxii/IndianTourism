Famous Places in Uttar Pradesh

Taj Mahal

A UNESCO World Heritage site in Agra, the Taj Mahal is a symbol of love and one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

Varanasi Ghats

The sacred ghats of Varanasi, including Dashashwamedh and Assi Ghat, are perfect for spiritual experiences along the Ganges.

Bara Imambara

A historic structure in Lucknow, known for its unique architecture and the incredible Bhool Bhulaiya (maze).

Ram Janmabhoomi

A sacred site in Ayodhya believed to be the birthplace of Lord Rama, now featuring a grand temple.

Krishna Janmabhoomi

A revered temple in Mathura marking the birthplace of Lord Krishna, a must-visit for devotees.

Jhansi Fort

A historic fort in Jhansi, symbolizing valor and the bravery of Rani Lakshmibai during the revolt of 1857.
