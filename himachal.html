Famous Places in Himachal Pradesh

Shimla

The capital of Himachal Pradesh, known for its colonial architecture, Mall Road, and lush green hills.

Manali

A popular hill station famous for adventure sports, Solang Valley, and Rohtang Pass.

Dharamshala

Home to the Dalai Lama, it offers serene monasteries and a glimpse of Tibetan culture.

Kullu

Known as the "Valley of Gods," Kullu is famous for its temples, festivals, and adventure sports.

Spiti Valley

A desert mountain valley with breathtaking landscapes and ancient monasteries.

Dalhousie

A charming hill station known for its colonial-era buildings and natural beauty.
